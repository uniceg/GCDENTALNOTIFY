How We Use Cookies

Medical Notify uses cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in and manage your session securely.
  • Remember your login details if you choose "Remember me" for faster access next time.
  • Enable secure features like password reset, OTP authentication, and admin/student access.
  • Store your preferences (such as language or accessibility settings, if available).
  • Improve your experience by analyzing how you use the site (e.g., page visits, navigation patterns).

We do not use cookies for advertising or to share your data with third parties. For more details, see our privacy policy or contact us at medicalnotify@gmail.com.
